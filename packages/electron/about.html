Nimbalyst

Version

A powerful, rich text editor built with Meta's Lexical framework, Nimbalyst is a local desktop app for:

  • Editing local markdown documents with a fast, clean, WYSIWIG editor
  • Collaborating with AI using your own Anthropic/OpenAI account or local models via LMStudio
  • Working across text, tables, code, lists, images, & excalidraw diagrams
